Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Innovation is the systematic practice of developing and marketing breakthrough products and services for adoption by customers.

Sustainability

Sustainability is the ability to meet the needs of the present without compromising the future.

Infrastructure

Infrastructure is the set of facilities and systems that serve a country, city, or other area.

Empowerment

Empowerment is the degree of autonomy and self-determination in people and in communities.
